Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
Starter (1 page): 2–5 days. Business (5 pages): 7 days. Professional (15 pages): 10 days. Enterprise: up to 14 days.
Do you provide domain and hosting?
Domain and hosting are not included but we guide you through purchasing them at cost price. We recommend Namecheap or Hostinger.
Will my website work on mobile phones?
Yes — every website we build is fully responsive and tested on Android, iPhone, tablets and desktop.
Can you integrate M-Pesa on my website?
Yes — we offer Daraja STK Push integration. You'll need a registered Safaricom Daraja account (we guide you through setup).
How many revisions are included?
Starter: 1 revision. Business: 2. Professional: 3. Additional revisions are KES 2,000 per round.
Do you offer social media separately?
Yes — standalone social media from KES 7,000/month. Minimum 3-month commitment applies.
